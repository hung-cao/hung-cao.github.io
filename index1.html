About me

I am a Ph.D. Student at the University of New Brunswick, studying at the People in Motion Laboratory (PIML). I am currently working under the supervision of Prof. Monica Wachowicz and Prof. Chiara Renso. I received B.Eng. degree (2011) in Computer Engineering from University of Information Technology, Vietnam National University - Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam) and M.Sc. degree (2015) in Computer Science from University College Dublin (Ireland) under the supervision of Dr. Nhien An Le Khac. Between 2005 and , I have served the University of Cyprus and the Open University of Cyprus as a Lecturer of Computer Science.

My research interests include Big Data Analytics; Internet of Things; Machine Learning; Cloud Computing; Edge Computing; and Fog Computing.
